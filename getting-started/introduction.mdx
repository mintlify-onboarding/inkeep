## Getting started

Inkeep is an AI-powered search and chat platform for developer products. With Inkeep, you can provide immediate answers to customer questions by leveraging your existing public and private content.

### Content ingestion

For a full list of supported sources, see [here](https://inkeep.com/pricing#content-sources).
